In 1986, Oscar Campana began his career as an interpreter working for SIU (Special Investigations Unit) and providing medical interpreting. Mr. Campana realized the high value of accurate interpreting services, especially in areas of jurisprudence and founded O.C. Interpreting in 1990. O.C. Interpreting's reputation for quality service led to widespread demand. Today O.C. Interpreting provides services world-wide and around the clock. Many insurance companies, legal firms and other multi-national corporations trust O.C. Interpreting with their most critical translation jobs.
